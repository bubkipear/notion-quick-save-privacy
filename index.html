Privacy Policy for Notion Quick Save

Effective Date: [Today's Date]

Information We Collect

Notion Quick Save only collects the minimal information necessary to provide its core functionality:

  • Notion Access Token: Stored locally in your browser to authenticate with your Notion account
  • Selected Page Preferences: Your chosen Notion page/database for saving content (stored locally)
  • Extension Settings: Your preferences like text popup toggle settings (stored locally)

How We Use Your Information

  • Notion Integration: Your access token is used solely to save content to your chosen Notion pages
  • Functionality: Settings are stored to remember your preferences across browsing sessions
  • No Tracking: We do not track your browsing activity or collect analytics

Information Storage and Security

  • All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's secure storage API
  • Your Notion access token is transmitted only directly to Notion's official API endpoints
  • We never store your data on our servers - there are no servers
  • No personal information is collected, stored, or transmitted to third parties

Data Sharing

  • We do not share, sell, or transmit your data to any third parties
  • Content you choose to save goes directly from your browser to your Notion account
  • We have no access to your Notion content or personal information

Your Rights

  • Uninstall: Remove the extension at any time to delete all stored preferences
  • Revoke Access: Disconnect your Notion account through Notion's settings at any time
  • Data Control: All your data remains under your control in your browser and Notion account

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as needed. Any changes will be reflected in the extension's store listing.
