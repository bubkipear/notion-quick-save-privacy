Privacy Policy for Notion Quick Save

Effective Date: December 2024

How This Extension Works

Notion Quick Save is a local-only extension that facilitates direct transfers between your browser and your accounts. We do not collect, store, or have access to your personal information.

Data Processing

When you use this extension, the following happens:

  • Text & URLs: Content you select is sent directly from your browser to your Notion account
  • Screenshots: Images are uploaded directly to Imgur to generate a URL, then that URL is sent to your Notion account
  • Authentication: Your Notion login token is stored locally in your browser only
  • Settings: Your preferences are stored locally in your browser only

Data Storage

  • No Backend: We have no servers and store no user data
  • Local Only: All settings and tokens remain in your browser
  • Direct Transfer: Content goes directly from your browser to your chosen services
About Screenshots: Screenshots are uploaded to Imgur and get a unique URL. While these URLs are technically "public," they are not indexed or discoverable - only someone with the exact URL can access your screenshot. Do not share these URLs if your screenshots contain sensitive information.

Third-Party Services

This extension transmits your data to the following third-party services:

  • Notion API (api.notion.com): All saved content is transmitted to and stored in your Notion workspace
  • Imgur API (api.imgur.com): Screenshots are uploaded and hosted publicly on Imgur's servers

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Authentication tokens and preferences are stored locally using Chrome's storage API
  • Remote Storage: Your content is stored in your Notion workspace and screenshots on Imgur's servers
  • Public Access: Screenshots become publicly accessible via Imgur URLs
  • No Encryption: Local storage is not encrypted beyond browser security measures

Permissions and Website Access

This extension requires the following permissions:

  • Access to all websites: Can read and capture content from any HTTPS website you visit
  • Active tab access: Reads content from your currently active browser tab
  • Browser storage: Stores authentication and preference data locally
  • Script injection: Injects content scripts into web pages for functionality
  • Tab information: Accesses tab URLs and titles for saving as sources

Your Rights and Controls

  • Uninstall: Remove the extension to delete all locally stored data
  • Revoke Access: Disconnect your Notion account through your Notion account settings
  • Data Management: Manage saved content through your Notion workspace
  • Screenshot Control: You control what screenshots are captured and saved

Data Retention

  • Local data is retained until you uninstall the extension
  • Content in Notion is retained according to Notion's data retention policies
  • Screenshots on Imgur are retained according to Imgur's policies

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as needed. Any changes will be reflected in the extension's store listing and this page.
